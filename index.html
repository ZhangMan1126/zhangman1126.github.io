About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. My research is in the field of Software Engineering with special interests on software testing of complex systems (such as Microservices and Cyber-Physical Systems) with AI-based and model-based techniques. All of my research contributions are fundamentally rooted in basic research within the field of software engineering.
